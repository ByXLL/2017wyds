John Naisbitt: "Belt and Road Initiative" construction is reshaping Globalization

2017-08-10 Source: Economic Daily 35

The Belt and Road construction is creating a new development framework, through joint investment in infrastructure construction, and the reconfiguration and strength of the world economic order and balance to reshape the globalization. In the foreseeable future, "The Belt and Road" will become the world's largest China cooperation platform, with a new model of globalization become a global leader in multilateral initiatives.

The day before, the world famous Futurist John Naisbitt in an "Economic Daily" reporter said, "Belt and Road Initiative" initiative will reshape the global new pattern, is a multi center global order, diversification of economic structure is an important pillar of stability, an important force of geopolitics and national situation.

John Naisbitt said that the current world order appeared obvious rift, the world development trend is reversed. Once led two times in Britain and the United States and Europe and become the global leader in inverse globalization; international organizations had once dominated the global economic governance reform is difficult in the remodeling process of globalization and global governance; who have driven the developed economies of free trade and regional economic integration are turning to protectionism. The consensus of western countries about globalization and free trade is fragmented, which undoubtedly undermines the ability to advance the free trade agenda. In this context, Chinese proposed "The Belt and Road" initiative, lays out a new way of interconnection, pragmatic attitude to "The Belt and Road" construction to benefit the needs of all parties, to meet the interests of all parties. It can be said, "The Belt and Road construction is creating a new development framework, through joint investment in infrastructure construction, and the reconfiguration and strength of the world economic order and balance to reshape the globalization.

John Naisbitt said, "Belt and Road Initiative" construction is a great initiative, aimed at creating a new balance of infrastructure, the development of new new trade routes and the establishment of a global partnership. "The Belt and Road" initiative for Africa and Europe radiation of six economic corridors. In 2016, China and Belt and Road Initiative "related to the national total trade is about $953 billion 590 million, accounting for Chinese total foreign trade amounted to 25.7%. "The Belt and Road" related to the national total population will reach 4 billion 400 million people, accounting for 62.55% of the total population of the world; the relevant national GDP sum of $21 trillion, equivalent to 28.6% of the total global GDP, it will produce a shock wave is hitherto unknown, in the global trade growth rate decreased from before the international financial crisis in 2008 7% to 2016 1.5% under the situation, the establishment of a new business will be the birth of a new trade order.

John Naisbitt believes that the next 10 years, "The Belt and Road building will become the engine of the development of Asia and the world. China's foreign investment surpassed foreign investment in China for the first time in 2015. So far, China of The Belt and Road "national investment totaled $51 billion 100 million, total foreign direct investment over the same period accounted for 12%, Chinese enterprises have more than 20 countries in the" The Belt and Road "on the construction of 56 economic and trade cooperation zone, a total investment of more than $18 billion 500 million, central trains run line has reached 46, arrived at the 29 European city, a total of more than 3000 passenger trains. "These figures show the fundamental attractiveness of The Belt and Road 'initiative is interoperability, Chinese is willing to share the advanced technology and management experience with relevant countries and regions, providing infrastructure, financial and environmental protection management services, closer cultural exchanges will help the parties to enhance mutual understanding, to deal with the economic and political uncertainties the challenges and better."

John Naisbitt said, "Belt and Road Initiative" initiative is also an important force for stability and the geopolitical situation in the country. Over the past few decades, western countries mainly through philanthropy and the transfer of funds to promote the economic development of underdeveloped areas, but most of these funds did not focus to the hands of the people, but the purchase of high-end real estate into some rulers in European countries, because of the money back to the donors. "Belt and Road Initiative" initiative is different, can use the interconnection to relevant countries and regions to create a safe environment and progress.

John Naisbitt believes that China has transformed from "world factory" into global investors and world investment banks. "The Belt and Road initiative will promote the global redistribution of wealth, which do not provide assistance, but to create suitable conditions to help "The Belt and Road" on people's work. In the foreseeable future, "The Belt and Road" will become the world's largest China cooperation platform, with a new model of globalization become a global leader in multilateral initiatives.
