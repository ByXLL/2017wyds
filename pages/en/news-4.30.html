The European Parliament: Europeans began to read "The Belt and Road" Initiative

2017-07-27 Source: Xinhua News Agency 35

Brussels in Belgium and the European Parliament building, is an ideal place for observation of Europe, pulse of trends, from the member countries of the European Union members held a meeting here and comment. So, how to treat the European members of China proposed "The Belt and Road" initiative?

The reporter found in the European Parliament the day before, "The Belt and Road" initiative for more than 3 years, many members of the European Parliament has become the focus of attention and study, they face the camera to talk about this initiative understanding and expectations.

The European Parliament European Friendship Group Chairman Neja Deva: today, the Europeans began to read the Belt and Road Initiative initiative, the initiative of the understanding of grand scale and significance.

Deva said, Belt and Road Initiative initiative aims to achieve interoperability of Eurasia and common development, which helps to realize the economic recovery in europe. Europeans realize that if the initiative is implemented, Europe's annual economic growth rate is expected to reach 6%, far higher than the current 1% or so.

The European Parliament member Anders Vistison of Denmark: Europe for The Belt and Road "positive attitude, and Chinese with Europe as the world's major economies, the need to strengthen cooperation in the field of trade and investment.

Weiss Thyssen believes that from a European perspective, Europe for "The Belt and Road" initiative to respond with some lag, politics is not ready, but this problem is solved.
