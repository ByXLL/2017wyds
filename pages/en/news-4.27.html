Dalian Port has become an important node of the maritime Silk Road

2017-08-10 Source: Liaoning Provincial Communications Department 35

In March 28th, the national development and Reform Commission, the Ministry of foreign affairs and the Ministry of Commerce jointly issued the vision and action to promote the construction of the Silk Road Economic Belt and the maritime Silk Road in twenty-first Century. Among them, 15 coastal ports, Dalian Port is among them, becoming the key node of the maritime silk road.

"The Belt and Road building is along the national grand vision of economic openness and cooperation. The Silk Road Economic Belt will focus on the smooth flow of China through Central Asia, Russia to Europe (Baltic Sea), China through Central Asia, West Asia to the Persian Gulf and the Mediterranean Sea, China to Southeast Asia, South Asia and the India ocean. "Vision and action" a clear position in the Belt and Road Initiative "and its key directions of cooperation, focus on Shaanxi, Inner Mongolia, involving Xinjiang, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaoning and other 18 provinces. The positioning of the three northeastern provinces is an important window for the construction of an open north. In twenty-first Century, the maritime Silk Road tried to move trade from China's coastal ports across the South China Sea to the India ocean and to Europe, from the Chinese coastal ports to the South China Sea and the South pacific. To achieve this goal, the document proposed to focus on the port as a node, and jointly build a smooth, safe and efficient transportation corridor. The key to strengthen Shanghai, Tianjin, Ningbo mentioned in Zhoushan, Guangzhou, Qingdao, Dalian and other 15 coastal city of port construction, become "The Belt and Road" especially in twenty-first Century the construction of the maritime Silk Road vanguard and main force.

In recent years, the Dalian Port group, while boosting the construction of the northeast old industrial base, has been integrated into the national strategic development. Through the use of port resources, routes and years of sea rail transportation experience, cooperative neighboring ports and to promote the "Liaoning over Europe", "Liao Mengou" and "Liaoning new Europe transport channel construction, promote the northeast and North China, Southern China, East China, Northwest Economic interconnection, and Europe, Southeast Asia, Japan and South Korea to promote the international trade, to create international multimodal transport channels, construction of the new Eurasian Continental bridge.

Favorable location advantage brings new development opportunity for port. Dalian Port as Liaoning coastal port leading, is opening up in the northeast of the bridgehead, with the advantages of the port promote the development of regional economy and the construction of "The Belt and Road" deep integration. At the same time, Dalian Port also has a prominent business advantage, China is facing the Pacific Ocean the nearest international port, more than 90% of the foreign trade goods in Northeast China through the import and export of Dalian Port, with more than 100 international and domestic routes, covering more than 300 ports and regions, have the basic conditions to build rail transport logistics channel.

As an important port in the northeast of opening to the outside world, Dalian Port undertakes the important responsibility to actively participate in the construction of "The Belt and Road". In the implementation of the The Belt and Road "construction, relying on the international route network, development of the the Yellow Sea sea and Bohai region regional air transport has made new progress. The container feeder service network covers 13 ports in Liaoning, Hebei, Shandong, yellow, Bohai and two domains, which provides important guarantee for the formation of a marine hub. In 2013, the Dalian Port group worked with COSCO to promote the development of the Northeast Arctic channel. This route embarks from Dalian Port, through Bering Strait, Arctic Ocean to Europe Rotterdam harbor, whole journey 7800 nautical mile, shorten 2800 nautical mile than traditional course. In addition, also focus on the global shipping network construction, conform to the marine low-carbon energy alliance of large-scale, resource trends, Bohai supply through ring transit, supply chain services to their own advantages, to further strengthen the foreign trade routes opened up, Japan and South Korea, Southeast Asia, expanding maritime trade channel.

"The Belt and Road" in planning cooperation put forward, will promote the construction of infrastructure, expand trade areas, deepen financial cooperation, promote cooperation in emerging industries, which will bring new opportunities for the development of the coastal ports.
