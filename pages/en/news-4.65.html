Shaanxi to expand the "The Belt and Road" higher education "circle of friends" give full play to the advantages of promoting educational cooperation

2017-08-10 Source: People's daily 35

"The cultural tour is my most beautiful memories, Chinese culture makes me intoxicated." Two years ago, the starting point of Northwestern University Turkey students Bai Zhenguo from the ancient Silk Road of Xi'an, visited 7 provinces in Western Chinese, travel nearly 30 thousand kilometers, known as the modern version of "Marco Polo".

"Shaanxi as the" frontier The Belt and Road 'opening to the west, is an important base of higher education in china." Shaanxi Provincial Department of education director Wang Jianli said, in recent years, Shaanxi give full play to the advantages of location and resources of science and education, always adhere to the priority of education development strategy, the internationalization of education as an important measure of development, to promote all-round education cooperation and exchanges. In Shaanxi, more and more "The Belt and Road" students participate in the construction of the country from here to the world, to the world about China story.

"I feel most fulfilled by trying my best to eliminate misunderstandings about China." "10 years ago, as a student, he came to China from Iran. Now, his students have stepped out of the campus and become professionals in the construction of the Silk Road Economic Belt," said, a foreign teacher at Xi'an International Studies University.

"To make the world aware of China, talent is the top priority." We train students not only to master relevant foreign languages, but also to have the ability to promote communication between civilizations. Xi'an International Studies University, Dean of the College of Oriental Languages and culture, Ma said that the college has more than 10 students were admitted to the Ministry of foreign affairs, the Ministry of culture and other ministries, 32 students admitted to the University of London, Peking University and other well-known institutions.

In order to expand exchanges and cooperation with Central Asian countries in the field of education, attract local students to study in Shaanxi, Xi'an International Studies University and Northwestern University also set up Central Asia institute. "In the past 10 years, the average number of students from Central Asian countries is about 300 per year, accounting for more than 60% of the total number of students studying abroad." Vice president of Northwestern University Chang Jiang said.
