Finland to build a "China response of The Belt and Road" initiative, the recent railway, shipping, civil aviation and other departments to actively seek docking, the parties jointly build a three-dimensional traffic pattern shows.

The Finland government investment promotion agency said recently, located in the southeast of Finland, Kazakhstan and Russia Kouvola not long ago and China signed an agreement will be opened from China Zhengzhou (or Xi'an) to korvola freight trains. This will add a branch to the central European map.

Project leader Simo Peivining said that if the trial run smoothly, is expected to be the fastest in the second half of this year opened. Then, from Zhengzhou (or Xi'an) to Kouvola as long as 10 days to 12 days.

"Westbound trains after the arrival of the goods at Kouvola can be sent to the Baltic coast, port by sea, and vice versa." COSCO Shipping Group Finland company general manager Chen Feng told Xinhua News Agency reporters. He said, "COSCO in The Belt and Road" under the framework of the establishment of the overseas development planning, is the Baltic rail transport in Europe to explore business opportunities.

Northern Europe is an important pulp producer in the world. Last year, Finland pulp producers signed ocean shipping contracts for 5 and a half years with cosco. COSCO has designed and built a world leading grocery wheel, regularly docked north of Finland and loaded with paper pulp.

At the same time, the "Silk Road in the air" expansion is also accelerating. Finland Airlines is currently running 6 non-stop Chinese routes, and the possibility has been seeking to open routes. Finnair CEO Pekka Waulamo recently in an interview with Xinhua News Agency reporters said: "Finnair response of western development and" The Belt and Road 'construction, according to the China business, hope to achieve a win-win situation."

In late April, the Chinese and Finnish authorities held talks to reach agreement on increasing navigation points, expanding transport capacity, eliminating the restrictions on the number of air transport enterprises, and signed a memorandum of understanding. Finland related departments said that the results of the talks to open Beijing non-stop flights to Rovaniemi possible.
