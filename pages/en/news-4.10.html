Xinjiang photovoltaic Silk Road opportunity seminar held ceremoniously

2017-08-10 Source: Silk Road Network 35

By China new energy chamber of Commerce, sponsored by the Committee and amorphous silicon solar photovoltaic, Le leaf TBEA hosted the Silk Road photovoltaic opportunities seminar "held in Xinjiang of Urumqi. In Xinjiang, 2000 years ago, the Silk Road from here through the "big economic corridor in the world's longest and most potential" (Silk Road Economic Belt) here as the core area; in Urumqi, the high temperature of more than 40 degrees, long time sunshine burn, "photovoltaic" two words in here is more shining. In the great historical opportunity to implement the "The Belt and Road" national strategy, with the construction of Xinjiang energy base, the meeting came into being, homeopathy, discussed under the background of new era, opportunities and challenges of the development of photovoltaic industry and the future trend. Especially recently frequently broke the photovoltaic power plant quality problems, let the public sustainability of PV have been questioned, the conference on "quality and benefits" photovoltaic power issues, industry related departments, organizations, experts and representatives of more than 180 people started a lively discussion, and brought wonderful to share. "In recent years, the quality of PV power stations in our country has been frequent, which has brought many negative effects to the development of the whole industry, so that more and more colleagues in the photovoltaic industry have begun to pay attention to quality problems." The vice president, new energy chamber of crystalline silicon PV branch of the chairman of the Committee Zhong Baoshen in the General Assembly said in his speech, the meeting will directly focus on quality issues. "If the components fall by more than 5% a year, at this rate, the station will be scrapped in 5 years," he said. It is urgent to solve the quality problem!" Subsequently, the the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region energy chamber of Commerce President Ding Zhiping, the National Federation of new energy chamber of Commerce vice president and Secretary General Ceng Shaojun, the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region professional trade federation vice president Guo Xiaoqing was in the General Assembly speech, expressed confidence in the development of Xinjiang new energy and improve the quality of photovoltaic power plants. As a result, the Silk Road opportunity seminar also officially opened.

The seminar is divided into two halves, the first half is mainly from the aspects of the development of photovoltaic industry, power plant China component technology and the quality of cut by the whole of the crystalline silicon PV of new energy chamber of Commerce Deputy Secretary General, Leye Photovoltaic Technology Co. Ltd. marketing department director Wang Yingge presided over the. The National Energy Research Institute researcher Wang Sicheng brought the original "analysis" the development trend of China photovoltaic industry and power quality improvement, he proposed the 2020 photovoltaic power plant capacity will exceed 150GW, which means that the future annual growth in more than 20GW. In addition, he pointed out that more than 50% of the power plant quality problems caused by component problems, we should pay more attention to product safety, reliability, the use of high quality components. Zhou Chunlan, an associate professor of the Institute of electrical engineering of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, introduced high efficiency batteries, processes and solutions for PERC batteries, heterojunction batteries, and back contact batteries. Leye Photovoltaic Technology Co. Ltd. technology senior manager Dr. He Song speech in the "comparative" single poly system theme, that affects the efficiency and advantage of single crystal in reliability, power generation and the temperature coefficient of the basic properties of single poly and a large number of experimental data. The future of single crystals will be better in the industry because of the decrease of power consumption, high conversion rate and high rate of power generation. In the network planning department of Xinjiang electric power company Statistics Director Han Xijiang shares a lot of data, he introduced the distribution of photovoltaic power plant in Xinjiang and the basic situation, new energy accounted for a peak of 35% in the network, there are some brownouts that need to be solved on the other hand.

The second half of the meeting by the general manager TBEA Xinjiang new energy Co. Ltd. assistant wells presided over china. The focus of the audience to focus on the new energy chamber of crystalline silicon PV and T Committee V Nande photovoltaic component credibility detection platform for the signing ceremony, the two sides signed the new energy in the National Federation of chamber of Commerce vice president and Secretary General Ceng Shaojun professional under the auspices of the two sides in the future will be committed to win-win cooperation, and jointly promote the healthy development of photovoltaic industry.

Professor Shen Wenzhong of the Shanghai Jiao Tong University study published "solar silicon heterojunction solar cell industry development" theme of his speech, he introduced to the participants with stage and development of heterojunction solar cell industry research. He pointed out that the N single crystal heterojunction battery will generate more than 10% of the ordinary silicon cells per watt, while the cost is only about 15%. In addition, the general manager Cui Yongxiang of Xi'an Longji clean energy limited company in the "high quality monocrystalline solar power photovoltaic power plant project" speech pointed out, especially in Xinjiang such a high temperature, crystal products have more advantages.
