Make concerted efforts to build the road of friendship between China and pakistan

2017-05-10 Source: People's daily 35

Aporta Bud is a quiet town in northern Pakistan, surrounded by mountains, shaded by cypress. The only road, the Karakoram Highway connecting channel between China and Pakistan after this. This highway is the most important economic lifeline for the northern Pakistani region. It is called "friendship road between China and Pakistan".

"Chinese brother's careful guidance, I am master of many skills, let me in the future can have in the world"

The highway was built in the last century, 60s and 70s. China and Pakistan builders together to fight for more than ten years, let's change thoroughfare. As a result of disrepair and heavy freight traffic, the lifeline of the local population is now on the upgrade. In order to create a more efficient and smooth traffic network, to better help the local economic and social development in the Chinese company organization, Pakistan builders are nervous fighting in the Karakoram Highway two project construction line. Into the construction site, Chinese to Chinese Luqiao Engineering Co. Ltd. (Luqiao Chinese) built its employees are working together in conjunction with more than 2000 Palestinian workers a mountain road. Smooth the roadbed, repair the retaining wall, build the bridge, and dig the tunnel... Construction on the spot is unfolding in an orderly way. In the boundless mountains, a new economic artery gradually emerged.

The 29 year old Abdul Gafour Aporta Bud is a local young man, as a father of 3 children, gafur feeling that they are the direct beneficiaries of the Karakoram Highway upgrading project. Cavour was working in Dubai, when he can go home once every two years. In that home near highway upgrade after the project started, he joined the team in the road without hesitation. "This is a great project for the benefit of future generations, through to participate, I can get a monthly salary of 30 thousand rupees ($1 about 104 Rupees) of steady income, and I in Dubai's income level, while also taking into account the family. Chinese brother's careful guidance, I am master of many skills, let me in the future can have in the world."

Driver Haji Kazam, who lives in Pakistan in northern Gilgit, he was 63 years old, over the past 20 years has been driving to and from the Karakoram Highway in Pakistan, between the northern region and the capital Islamabad to transport rice and clothing, daily necessities, witnessed the great development zone along the highway to the. Kazam said that before the fruits and vegetables and other agricultural products will be damaged in transit, the local people are often helpless. "A period of Karakoram Highway expansion project completed, for us to save more than half of the travel time and transportation costs, now I can put fresh Vegetable Fruit soon to the north, it is difficult to imagine in the past." The improvement of traffic conditions has made the products of northern Pakistan more convenient for sale and increased the income of the local people.

China road Qiaoka La Kunlun highway two (Hervey Leigh Jan to taco special paragraph project manager) Wang Benqian said to the reporter: "has very important significance to the Karakoram Highway, Luqiao Chinese constructionpersonnel will inherit and carry forward the fine tradition, give full play to their professional dedication, Chinese road construction quality, construction of a high-quality highway. It is hoped that the road will further help the local people improve their lives after the completion of the highway."

Pakistan Economic Corridor under the framework of the transport infrastructure projects smoothly, can add new vigor to the economic development of Pakistan and Pakistan, will help further enhance the domestic level of interoperability, play advantage, make it become a bridge and link between neighboring countries and regions of the world, promoting the common development of regional economy. Pakistan Economic Corridor is "The Belt and Road" flagship project, thank Chinese proposed "The Belt and Road" initiative, create an important opportunity to promote Bazhong and the countries of the region to achieve joint development and win-win cooperation.
