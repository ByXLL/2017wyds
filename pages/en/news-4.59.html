Zheng He is China's great Ming Dynasty navigator and diplomat, at the age of thirty-four, led by the world's largest fleet of seven expeditions, which lasted twenty-eight years, creating a maritime Silk Road of China to the more than 30 Asian and African countries and regions. Since the Ming Dynasty Yongle three years (1405) to 2015, Zheng He has been to the West for the past 610 years.

Yongle three years (AD 1405) in July, Zheng He appointed by emperor, led by the giant ship 62 ships, soldiers and staff of more than 2 people, from Suzhou Liujiagang (now Jiangsu Taicang Liuhe Town) of the East China Sea, South China Sea, China returned last two years and two months after. This is the history of mankind's largest sailing fleet, is the history of mankind's greatest sailing voyage, its scale, number, scope, not only in the Chinese maritime history is the first time, and has no precedent in the world maritime history.
