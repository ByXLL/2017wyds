China and Philippines hold investment forum to promote bilateral cooperation

2017-08-10 Source: Beijing Morning News Network 35

China jointly organized by the Ministry of Commerce and the Philippines trade and industry "in the Philippine trade and Investment Forum" held in Manila on the 24 day, from the governments of the two countries, nearly 300 representatives of financial institutions and enterprises to discuss how to promote bilateral cooperation.

Philippines trade and Industry Minister Ramon Lopez said in his speech, Philippines is one of the gateway to the Southeast Asian market in recent years, has developed into the association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) is one of the most valuable investment. As the rotating presidency of ASEAN this year, Philippines will make efforts to promote the negotiation process of the regional comprehensive economic partnership agreement (RCEP).

Chinese ambassador to Philippines Zhao Jianhua said that Philippines is "The Belt and Road initiative supporters, President Duthel Te will be held in May this year to participate in the" China Belt and Road Initiative International Cooperation Forum, Philippines will become an important part of twenty-first Century the construction of the maritime Silk Road branch.

According to the Embassy in the Philippines China business department, China sent in recent years, the largest trade delegation to China to participate in the forum, and more than 130 representatives from more than 40 enterprises in the Philippines enterprise of business negotiation.

China Aviation Technology International Holdings Ltd. deputy general manager of Civil Aviation Department Duan Baorong told reporters that the company plans to China aircraft, helicopters and other civil aviation products to overseas markets, hope to help the interconnection of Philippines and other Southeast Asian countries to achieve better, make a contribution to the local economic and social development.

It is understood that the investment forum will focus on China and the Philippines in infrastructure, capacity cooperation, industrial park construction, trade, e-commerce, building materials and new energy and other fields of cooperation opportunities.
