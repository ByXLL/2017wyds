"The Belt and Road" to stimulate the vitality of the city

2017-08-10 Source: People's Daily - People's Daily Overseas Edition 35

Since 2013, President Xi Jinping proposed to build a Silk Road Economic Belt and twenty-first Century Maritime Silk Road major initiative, "The Belt and Road" as the keyword search quickly rising in the Internet search engine, has become the development strategy of the world.

In March 28th, the national development and Reform Commission, the Ministry of foreign affairs and the Ministry of Commerce jointly issued the "vision and action to promote the construction of the Silk Road Economic Belt and the maritime Silk Road in twenty-first Century", which aroused strong repercussions both at home and abroad. To build a "The Belt and Road" is Chinese initiative, and is also the common wish and Chinese along the country's.

Today, the area China are positive actions, in accordance with the "overall blueprint The Belt and Road construction, according to their characteristics, exert their own advantages, to find a meeting point to participate, and write a new chapter to build a" The Belt and Road. This newspaper selected several representative from the city, they reported in advance "The Belt and Road" construction ideas, measures and development prospects, "The Belt and Road" strategy inspired by the economic and social development potential.

Actively participate in the national "The Belt and Road" strategy, Sanya is a very rare opportunity." Hainan provincial Party committee, Sanya municipal Party committee secretary Zhang Qi said, to seize the opportunity, in build an upgraded version of Sanya economy; in speeding up the construction of diversification of industry as the leading tourism; in the construction of a full range of international tropical seaside tourist city boutique.

Sanya mayor Wu Yanjun said that with the opening of the central state fishing port, Sanya will realize the separation of passenger, cargo and fishing ports from the three ports. After the opening of the fishing port, 800 large, medium and small fishing boats can be moored, traded, supplied and sheltered from the wind. The first phase project of Sanya Nanshan port project has been completed and put into use. The future port of Sanya will concentrate on berthing passenger vessels for the development of marine tourism services.

In "The Belt and Road" 'construction, Sanya should be based on their own advantages, accelerate the transformation of international tourism elements, the construction of excellent tourism projects, lodging, travel and entertainment comprehensive upgrading." Wu Yanjun says.

Sanya carries out visa for 26 tourists from more than 5 countries through travel agencies, which is practiced in 3 countries, such as Germany, where more than 2 people can enjoy it. Since the beginning of this year, Sanya has stepped up its efforts in opening up international routes. March 29th, Sanya to Osaka, Japan, round-trip flights opened. During the year, 6 international routes, such as "Sanya Shanghai Moscow", "Sanya Singapore", "Sanya Laos", and "Sanya Vietnam", will also be opened.

And a comprehensive upgrade, showing Sanya actively integrate into the "The Belt and Road "strategy, to create an international tourist city boutique ambition.

This "The Belt and Road" strategy, Shaanxi city of Xi'an province and the first world together again for China Unicom road. Through the railway, highway, aviation, oil and gas pipelines, optical fiber, communication multi-dimensional form, a large open channel connected to the Tongjiang sea, land and air transport, combined with the actual situation, seamless, reproduce the "international trade" spectacular.
