Xinjiang Fujian will be built "The Belt and Road" core area

The national development and Reform Commission, the Ministry of foreign affairs, the Ministry of Commerce jointly issued the "March 28th push to build Silk Road Economic Belt and twenty-first Century Maritime Silk Road vision and action", China advocated "The Belt and Road" is a programmatic document.

The national development and Reform Commission, the Ministry of foreign affairs, the Ministry of Commerce jointly issued the "March 28th push to build Silk Road Economic Belt and twenty-first Century Maritime Silk Road vision and action", China advocated "The Belt and Road" is a programmatic document. The document pointed out that Xinjiang and Fujian will be built into the core area of The Belt and Road ".

The file of "The Belt and Road" lines are made clear, Silk Road Economic Belt key Chinese unimpeded through Central Asia, Russia and Europe (Baltic); Chinese via central Asia, Western Asia to the Persian Gulf and the Mediterranean; China to Southeast Asia, South Asia, India ocean. In twenty-first Century, the maritime silk road was focused on the sea from China's coastal ports to the South China Sea and the India ocean, extending to Europe, from the Chinese coastal ports to the South China Sea and the South Pacific ocean.

The file has carried on the division to the Chinese local participation in the "The Belt and Road" in the construction of the role and status of. For Xinjiang, the document calls for the unique regional advantages of Xinjiang and the important window role of opening to the west, deepening exchanges and cooperation with Central Asian countries, South Asia, Western Asia and other countries, and creating the core area of the Silk Road Economic belt. In twenty-first Century, the heart of the maritime silk road was in Fujian.

The document pointed out that the way to build a "The Belt and Road" is to target coordination, policy communication, is open and pluralistic cooperation process. China is willing to work with countries along, enrich and perfect "the content and methods of cooperation "The Belt and Road", jointly formulated the timetable for the roadmap, actively docking along the national development planning and regional cooperation.
