The two countries signed an agreement to promote the "Chamber of Commerce The Belt and Road" cooperation

Silk Road International Chamber of Commerce to promote the activities of 25 days in Slovakia's capital Bratislava closing, two chamber of Commerce signed a strategic cooperation agreement to deepen cooperation initiatives under the framework of the "The Belt and Road", delegates said Slovakia will become an important partner of The Belt and Road cooperation.

During the event, the Silk Road International Chamber of Commerce to the business circles of Slovakia introduced the "Silk Road International Development Fund", "Silk Road" and other international cooperation projects, and with the World Federation of chamber of Commerce signed a cooperation in the "The Belt and Road" under the framework of the strategic cooperation agreement.

The World Federation of chambers of Commerce, chairman of the Slovakia Federation of industry and Commerce Chairman Peter mihok said China is to restart the five thousand years of history of the Silk Road, which is an important route for a connection between Asia and the European Economic and trade activities. He stressed that this is an important cooperation between the business community of Slovakia and the International Chamber of Commerce of the silk road.

The Silk Road, the president of the International Chamber of Commerce Lv Jianzhong said that Eastern Europe is "The Belt and Road" in Europe, Slovakia is located in Central Europe, the four corners of the world thoroughfare, is a bridge connecting Eastern and Western Europe market and the Balkans portal. Slovakia has witnessed steady economic growth in recent years and has great potential for development. The government departments have adopted a series of measures to introduce foreign capital and improve the investment environment, including tax relief, subsidies and laws and regulations.

At the same time, Lv Jianzhong stressed that in the "The Belt and Road" construction, the association of national business should give full play to the advantages of close contact with the enterprise, widely collect and reflect the opinions and suggestions to the government in a timely manner, to assist the government to carry out macroeconomic policy communication, strive for more policy support for the enterprises to participate along road construction. At the same time, give full play to the role of business associations resources, promote the facilities of China Unicom and trade flow, help enterprises along the country based on their own advantages, to fully tap in infrastructure construction, import and export trade, industrial investment, energy resources and financial cooperation in the areas of potential.
