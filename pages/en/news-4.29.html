Macao will fully participate in the "The Belt and Road" construction

2017-08-10 Source: China Military Network 35

Cui Shian, chief executive of the Macao Special Administrative Region (SAR), returned to Macao after attending the opening ceremony of the 2015 annual conference of the Boao forum for Asia (28). He said that Macao will fully participate in the "The Belt and Road" construction, make the greatest efforts for national development, and to promote a new round of development zone.

Cui Shian said that President Xi Jinping attended the opening ceremony of the 2015 annual meeting of the Boao forum for Asia and delivered a keynote speech. He said he was fully encouraged and encouraged by President Xi's initiative. The Macao SAR government as early as last year have already submitted a proposal to the Central District of Macao to participate in the national version of the "13th Five-Year" plan, clearly expressed the hope to actively participate in the construction of The Belt and Road "will. The recently published 2015 annual policy report also clearly stated, will be dedicated to connecting The Belt and Road construction direction, strengthen economic and trade exchanges with ASEAN countries and cultural exchanges.

He stressed that the SAR government attaches great importance to and will fully cooperate with the 28 published "to promote the construction of the Silk Road Economic Belt and the twenty-first Century Maritime Silk Road vision and action" on the Macao SAR's participation path. The Macao SAR has unique advantages and is involved in this major strategy. Among them, Macao's tourism industry, the pharmaceutical industry has the potential to participate in, and as a "China SAR cooperation with the Portuguese speaking countries trade service platform", should be able to form a superimposed effect in promoting the "The Belt and Road construction. In addition, Macao large number of aspiring national service and the SAR of Returned Overseas Chinese, will also be unique elements in the national strategy.

He said the government will accelerate the research of Macao how to play their own advantages, help "The Belt and Road" construction, and to promote a new round of development zones, to achieve their own progress.

He said that the Macao SAR government attaches great importance to the issue of joining the Asian infrastructure investment bank, and is actively supporting and coordinating the important strategic arrangement of the country.
