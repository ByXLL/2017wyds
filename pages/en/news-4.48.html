Xi Jinping announced the construction of The Belt and Road Chinese to do these things

2017-08-10 Source: People's daily 35

This morning, "The Belt and Road International Cooperation Forum opened in Beijing. President Xi Jinping attended the opening ceremony and delivered a keynote speech to the world, a comprehensive exposition of "The Belt and Road" initiative. How to build Belt and Road Initiative "China will contribute? The whole world cares. In the speech, Xi Jinping announced: Chinese to New Silk Road fund capital is 100 billion yuan RMB, held in 2018 from the China International Import Expo, launched the "The Belt and Road" technology innovation action plan...Over the next few years, China will do a lot of things to look at.

Xi Jinping pointed out that China and the relevant countries of the railway sector will sign deepening cooperation agreement between China and europe. And pointed out that these cooperation projects will be launched at an early date, early results.

At present, China Central Europe class has opened a total of 3000 lines, covering 21 provinces and autonomous regions, reaching 29 cities in 11 European countries. In the future, deepening cooperation between China and EU will undoubtedly benefit the countries and people along the Taiwan strait.

Xi Jinping pointed out that Chinese will increase support for the "The Belt and Road" construction funds, additional funds of 100 billion yuan to the Silk Road Fund, encourage financial institutions to carry out overseas RMB funds, the scale is expected to be approximately 300 billion yuan.

At the same time, Chinese National Development Bank, the export import bank will provide 250 billion yuan and 130 billion yuan RMB equivalent special loans to support The Belt and Road "capacity, infrastructure construction, financial cooperation.

In addition, Chinese will also work with the Asian infrastructure investment bank, the new BRICs Development Bank, the world bank and other multilateral development agencies to support the "The Belt and Road" project, with all parties to jointly develop The Belt and Road financing guidelines.

Xi Jinping announced that during the current forum, China will sign an agreement on economic and trade cooperation with more than 30 countries and negotiate free trade agreements with the countries concerned.

From 2014 to 2016, Chinese with "The Belt and Road trade along the country a total of more than $3 trillion. Chinese enterprises have built 56 economic and trade cooperation zones in more than 20 countries. In the future, with the signing of more economic and trade cooperation agreements and free trade agreements, trade and investment will be more convenient for all countries concerned.

In his speech, Xi Jinping announced that China will hold the China International Import fair from 2018.

Last year, in the international market demand downturn, in the "The Belt and Road" cooperation, economic and trade cooperation between China and the countries have made gratifying achievements. From the General Administration of customs data, last year, China's imports of goods 10 trillion and 490 billion yuan, of which, China imported from The Belt and Road along the country was 2 trillion and 400 billion yuan, up 0.5%. Announcing the launching of the China International Import fair is a major positive boost for China's trade and investment facilitation with all relevant countries.

Xi Jinping said, Chinese is willing to strengthen innovation and cooperation with other countries, launched the "The Belt and Road" science and technology innovation action plan, carry out scientific and technological and cultural exchanges, joint laboratory, science and Technology Park cooperation, technology transfer 4 action.
