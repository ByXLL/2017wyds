United Nations land, sea and Silk Road City Alliance settled in Chongqing

2017-07-27 Source: Yuzhong newspaper 35

June 20th, the United Nations land and Sea Silk Road City Alliance, smart city and emerging industries Committee settled in Chongqing signing ceremony. The United Nations Sea Silk Road City Association honorary president Long Yongtu, the Silk Road City Union UN director Liang Dan, municipal government deputy secretary general Zhang Zhikui, deputy secretary of municipal Party Committee Propaganda Department, municipal government information office director Zhou Bo, Yuzhong party secretary Tang Yingyu attended the signing ceremony. United Nations land and Sea Silk Road League Secretary General Zhao Yongli, deputy secretary of the district Party committee and district governor Hu Wantai speech.

It is understood that the Silk Road city alliance is the United Nations United Nations project by the United Nations Industrial Development Organization, the United Nations Development Programme, the United Nations South South Cooperation Office, UNESCO, the UN World Tourism Organization such as the five United Nations agencies and the Ministry of Commerce China international economic and technical exchange center jointly launched that is a specialized agency within the United Nations system only "The Belt and Road coordination in international affairs. The United Nations Sea Silk Road City Alliance smart city and emerging industries is the United Nations Committee of the Silk Road City Alliance special approved the establishment, to promote the countries along the Silk Road, the government and enterprises in the city and folk wisdom of emerging industries in the field of comprehensive and in-depth cooperation non-profit specialized agencies.

Hu Wantai said that the current Yuzhong is to seize the country "The Belt and Road", the Yangtze River Economic Belt strategic layout and the new Chongqing strategic interoperability demonstration projects and other major opportunities, and actively integrate into the global economic integration process, to build the city of quality and vitality in a more open view of the area. The United Nations Sea Silk Road City Alliance and the wisdom of the city committee to promote the coordination and emerging industries along the Silk Road city resources, investment and trade, promote cultural exchanges play an important role in the wisdom of the city and in-depth cooperation in the field of emerging industry. Hope that the signing for the new starting point, the formation of good relations of cooperation between the United Nations and the League of the Silk Road City Smart City and emerging industries Commission and Chongqing, Yuzhong, and jointly build the Silk Road between countries in the wisdom of the city and the emerging industry in the field of investment, technology and industrial cooperation platform for the global industry resources Chinese and foreign investment institutions and social capital to set up a communication bridge, seamless docking industry and capital, and accelerate the project, provide a new platform for the innovation and development of Yuzhong's transformation and upgrading, and make more contribution to the development of Chongqing.

Zhao Yongli said that the United Nations Sea Silk Road City Alliance smart city and emerging industries Commission headquarters in Chongqing, is the value of the comprehensive advantages of the only municipality in Western China location characteristics, economic development, industry, science and technology culture, ecological environment, human environment and environmental aspects of the government. The committee will coordinate the "The Belt and Road along national and regional resources, in the wisdom of the city and emerging industries, to promote economic and trade cooperation, industrial investment, financing, cultural exchanges, personnel exchanges, etc.
