Melinda Gates: "Belt and Road Initiative" initiative will bring the world a lot of benefits

2017-08-10 Source: People's daily 35

Over the past few decades, China has made remarkable achievements in the eradication of poverty and the fight against disease, which is inseparable from cooperation with many international institutions. Recently, the co chairman of Melinda Gates's visit to China during the Bill and Melinda Gates foundation, the foundation and the China national health and Family Planning Commission jointly launched a new project to deepen bilateral cooperation in health poverty and AIDS prevention and control etc. Melinda said in an exclusive interview with reporters in Beijing, "in the field of poverty alleviation and health," China has rich experience and expertise, worthy of world share."

"I came to China for the first time in 1995, and China has made great progress in the past 20 years, and I believe the whole world has seen that progress.". China has lifted more than 700 million people out of poverty - that's an astronomical figure!" Melinda told reporters.

Co founded by famous industrialist and philanthropist Bill Gate and his wife Melinda Gates foundation is the world's largest private foundation since its inception in 2000, its donations totaled about 30000000000 dollars, the project distributed in more than 100 countries and regions in the world. In 2007, the foundation began to set up the Beijing office, which has carried out a great deal of work in China and witnessed the development and change of china. Melinda said that what she and Bill were most proud of was the foundation's consistent support for the Chinese government's achievement of poverty alleviation goals. "We are not only their own testimony, and it has been told to the world, great achievements made in Chinese achieve the UN Millennium Development Goals, and progress in reducing poverty, China significant progress on maternal and neonatal mortality and - this is the whole world."

China has made great achievements in the fight against malaria. Melinda pointed out that China had 60 million malaria patients in the past, and now only a few thousand cases a year. China should extend its valuable experience in malaria control to other countries and regions of the world, such as Burma and African countries, and teach them how to combat malaria and other diseases.

Melinda's professional field is technology, and she has been concerned about how technological innovation will affect the future development of society, and how to serve the sustainable development. She stressed that China is leading the world in the field of mobile payments. Even if you live in a rural area, you can save a few dollars a day by shopping online. If your spouse has a job in the city, you can easily transfer money to your living in the countryside by phone. At the same time, mobile payments are booming all over the world and are changing people's lives dramatically. China is also strengthening its cooperation with other countries in the field of mobile payments.

Chinese mentioned "The Belt and Road" initiative, Melinda said, "Belt and Road Initiative" initiative will give the world bring many benefits, it will benefit not only Chinese people. She is also very concerned about South South cooperation initiatives and how these initiatives help African countries. Bill and I go to Africa three or four times a year, and we feel the spirit of entrepreneurship in africa. What excited me was that the new bus system we saw in Tanzania came from china."

Melinda believes that through the promotion of China's expertise in poverty alleviation, such as providing farmers with appropriate agricultural machinery, so that farms get a better harvest, may allow millions of Africans out of poverty. For example, China's research and development in the field of health is progressing rapidly, which will benefit people all over the world.
