International: the BRIC cooperation to promote the construction of "The Belt and Road"

2017-07-27 Source: China News Network 35

Three days of the BRIC countries, political parties, think tanks and civil society organizations forum closed in Fuzhou on 12. International Liaison Department of the CPC Central Party Secretary Song Tao said that the BRIC countries, think tanks and civil society organizations to promote the BRICs cooperation has become the pioneer of the New South South cooperation, which is an important aspect of the work to promote the construction of "The Belt and Road".

Song Tao delivered a speech entitled "consensus among all parties and deepening cooperation among the BRICs" at the closing ceremony. He said that the BRIC countries must undertake the important mission The Belt and Road "construction, to help developing countries to achieve common development, promote the rise of developing countries, become the driving force of South South cooperation to a higher level, a deeper level of development.

Song Tao delivered a speech entitled "consensus among all parties and deepening cooperation among the BRICs" at the closing ceremony. He said that the BRIC countries must undertake the important mission The Belt and Road "construction, to help developing countries to achieve common development, promote the rise of developing countries, become the driving force of South South cooperation to a higher level, a deeper level of development.

Song Tao said that the BRICs cooperation has been developed for ten years, but it is still a new thing from the historical point of view, how to develop and deepen cooperation is still a lack of ready-made experience for reference, we must listen to the views and recommendations, also need the government, political parties, civil society organizations, think tanks and other aspects of power, actively support participation and close cooperation. BRICs political parties, think tanks and civil society organizations should play their respective advantages, and strive to nurture, foster and strengthen the BRICs cooperation force, and constantly consolidate the public opinion and social foundation of the BRICs cooperation.

Song Tao said that as an important group of representatives of developing countries, the BRIC countries will continue to explore the road of development suited to their own national conditions, to help developing countries to strengthen capacity building in developing countries, new partners to promote equality and mutual trust, mutual benefit, mutual solidarity and further play an important role. The BRICs think tanks and political parties, civil society organizations should closely follow the new situation of international cooperation, continue to explore new ways to promote South South cooperation through the BRICs cooperation, active participation, to promote the "The Belt and Road" construction, new impetus and vigor unceasingly for the BRIC cooperation.

Song Tao believes that the current BRIC cooperation is at a critical stage of development, both facing rare opportunities, but also face complex challenges. We should promote BRICs cooperation as a new practitioner of globalization, a facilitator of new global governance, and a pioneer of New South South cooperation.

Song Tao said that the BRIC countries parties, think tanks and civil society organizations to actively support the BRIC countries participating in the economic globalization, maintain and develop an open world economy; actively advocated to jointly build and share new ideas for global governance principles, actively undertake the responsibility of leading the global governance to promote the brics.
