The radiation force of ancient Silk Road

2017-08-04 Source: China Youth Network 46

Taking a broad view of the history of the ancient Chinese Silk Road, it can be roughly divided into two historical stages. From Zhang Qian in the Western Han Dynasty to the western regions (138 BC - 126 BC), to the Ming Dynasty, Zheng He's western voyages (1405 -1430) were the first stage.

At this stage, the original land is "Silk Road", basically in the Central Plains of Changan (Xi'an) or Luoyang as the starting point, to the west by the western countries, in the Central Asian countries in the region, the most famous is the Western Han Dynasty Zhang Qian western and Eastern Han Dynasty beyond to the western regions. Chen Cheng of the Ming Dynasty visited the western regions in 1370, 1375, 1377 and 1383, and Hou Xianceng made five missions to the western regions in four. That is to say, the "Silk Road" of land never stopped and interrupted until the Ming dynasty.

Later, the maritime "Silk Road" began to develop, the most famous is the Ming Dynasty Zheng He seven times to the west. The Ming Dynasty also sent Zhang Qian, Tan Sheng, Yin Qing, Yang Xin, Zhou Hang and other rate fleet several times to the west.
