"I have witnessed the great changes in cross border shipping between Asia and Europe" - a story of a Huoerguosi port worker

2017-08-10 Source: People's daily 35

8 afternoon, Kazakhstan is a state visit of President Xi Jinping, and President Nazarbayev, has wired Lianyungang and Kazakhstan logistics cooperation base and the "Huoerguosi Special Economic Zone East Gate" dry port, about Kazakhstan cross-border transport cooperation. At this time, the 27 year old Bdukhov Gevale of Kazakhstan, goosebumps.

As the hot Kazakhstan Huoerguosi Wali international border cooperation center of the national new stock company spokesman, has been working here for 5 years. These 5 years, but also the cooperation center from scratch, from small to large 5 years. He said: "the Huoerguosi port is an important pivot in cross-border transport between Asia and Europe, where I have witnessed the great changes brought about by cross-border transportation between Asia and Europe."

The formal operation in April 18, 2012, the cooperation center of history, his hot performance.

In December 2002, President Nazarbayev of the Kazakhstan made a tentative plan for his visit to china. Two years later, China and Kazakhstan signed a cooperation framework agreement on the establishment of the Huoerguosi international border cooperation center. In July 1, 2007, the two sides officially opened cooperation center. We hope that through the creation of the center and its role as an international trade free port and an international thoroughfare, China Kazakhstan economic and trade cooperation will be spread to Western Asia, South Asia, Eastern Europe and all European countries.

Gevale told reporters, from the official operation to the present 5 years, his biggest feeling is that "cross-border transport, border trade, so that the town of Kazakhstan thoroughly reborn."

In December 2012, the heat from the East Kazakhstan state Wall-E home trek to Huoerguosi port to find a job. "Get off, I'm silly, here, in addition to China and Kazakhstan border station two towers, nothing, is a desert." Eventually the heat brick stayed. "I am sure that Huoerguosi will become the hub of cross-border transportation and border trade between China and kazakhstan. At least my Chinese can be used here."

The boy apparently made the right decision. Over the past 5 years, he sees a desert has become today in manufacturing, transit of goods, financial services and entertainment and other functions for the integrated international trade center, a demonstration zone of economic and trade exchanges between the countries of the SCO framework.

At the same time, modern office buildings, hotels and shops have sprung up. According to the plan, in the future, around the cooperation center, a new city will be built - the city of light. "Metro planning population of 100 thousand people, such a large-scale city, 5 years ago did not dare to imagine." Gevale sighed.
