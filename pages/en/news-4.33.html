In the construction of the Malaysia East Coast Railway project started along the benefit of more than 4 million 400 thousand people

2017-08-10 Source: People's daily 35

In August 9th, Malaysia Pahang state capital of Kuantan, she and her husband are law Ting Rizal east coast railway line project stopped on the sand table simulation. Lai Sa's home in Terengganu, he expects the railway opened to traffic, the way back home is more convenient and more comfortable. Ni Ting just submitted the "Sino Malaysia railway personnel training cooperation plan" application at the scene, hoping to have the opportunity to participate in the construction of the East Coast Railway Project in the future.

By the China Communications Construction Co (hereinafter referred to as "China built") the construction of the Malaysia East Coast Railway Project groundbreaking ceremony in Malaysia in August 9th held in Kuantan, capital of pahang. The project contract amount of about 550 ringgit (about 85 billion 900 million yuan), is the largest engineering China enterprises overseas under construction, is the next few years in Malaysia and Southeast Asia's most popular super engineering. The project adopts the national standard passenger and freight standard electrified railway, designed passenger train speed of 160 kilometers per hour, freight train speed of 80 kilometers, the estimated duration is 7 years. This is the first time that China's railway standards entered Malaysia. It was warmly welcomed by local governments, enterprises and people.

Every year, Eid al Fitr is the happiest and most annoying day for Cliff in his hometown in Kelantan: "happiness is that you can get together with your family. The trouble is that you may spend one day and one night on the road."

Kelantan is located at the northernmost end of the east coast of Malaysia. It can only take the highway from the capital Kuala Lumpur. "It takes 8 hours to drive normally and doubles during holidays." Cliff dreamed of being able to return home from Kuala Lumpur by train, just as convenient as when traveling in china.

Harif is a step by step approach to realize the dream.

In August 9th, the Malaysia East Coast Railway project, with a total length of about 688 kilometers, was officially launched under the witness of the representatives of the governments of China and malaysia. The place of the ceremony in Kuantan near a Loess ground. In 2024, a train terminal would be built on the yellow ground, becoming one of more than 20 sites along the East Coast Railway project to benefit the local population.

The East Coast Railway is an important milepost in eastern Malaysia project to promote development, promote regional interoperability, will greatly promote the economic development after the completion of the East Coast Economic Zone and the East Coast states, promote the interconnection of Kuala Lumpur and east coast between the lifting position of Malaysia in Southeast Asia and influence.
