Dialogue between Chinese and Croatia Writers: the silk road

2017-08-10 Source: Xinhua News Agency 35

12, at the thirty-ninth book exhibition in Croatia, Chinese writer Xu Zechen and the Croatia writer Asna Horvat about the Silk Road dialogue attracted many listeners.

In the dialogue, Horvat introduced his latest novel, VILIJUN. The novel takes Marco Polo along the Silk Road to China as the main line and describes what he saw and heard along the way. She said that when she visited China in 2010, China's long history, rich culture and rapid economic development inspired her creative inspiration. The title "VILIJUN" to follow Marco Polo in his famous travels in the creation of the million word, but may be of great China than horses BOLUO and rich description is more powerful.

Xu Zechen said, in the middle of the thirteenth Century Marco Polo can put the story of a country to the other side of the world, is the world's first international writers; international cultural exchange the way he opened, can be described as "Silk Road Cultural field".

Croatia scholars believe that Marco Polo was born in the island of Chula Carle kernan. Xu Zechen said that it was Marco Polo and the silk road that linked China and Croatia, two countries far apart. Today, as globalisation grows, it is important to talk about cultural sages, Marco Polo and the ancient Silk road.

In the dialogue, Xu Zechen introduced his works of Lao She's literature prize in 2014, "Jerusalem". He said that everyone has his own Jerusalem, and that China is Marco Polo's Jerusalem.

The dialogue between the two writers won enthusiastic applause from the audience. They hoped that both the cultural circles and publishing circles would enhance cooperation and introduce more translations of excellent Chinese works to the people of Croatia.

Since its opening on the 8 day, the Croatia Book Fair has been hosted by the Beijing Municipal Press and Publication Administration of radio and television, and the visitors to the Chinese exhibition hall, which is hosted by the China National Book Import Export Corporation, have been in an endless stream. The Chinese booth brought more than 300, more than 500 books, social science, science and technology, involving literature, children and other categories of books, Chinese books and includes very Chinese characteristics. During the exhibition, China Pavilion also organized tea ceremony and calligraphy and other activities. A visitor said: "here is not only elegant, and tea and ink. Chinese culture really makes me drunk."
