Fujian Anxi tea: "Belt and Road Initiative" fragrance China symbol

2017-07-17 Source: Xinhua News Agency 46

Fujian Anxi tea, since ancient times is the "maritime Silk Road" to the world, become a Marine Silk Road Road's symbol Chinese unrecoginized. Now, by virtue of carrying "The Belt and Road" Dongfeng, Anxi to speed up the pace of going out, especially in recent years, Fujian inspection and Quarantine Bureau and local government to promote the national export of tea quality safety demonstration area and the national organic product certification demonstration area construction, open up "with the way along the country market obvious effect.

Reporters from the Quanzhou inspection and Quarantine Bureau was informed that the first half of 2017, the Bureau of tea export supervision and inspection of 977 tons, 9 million 380 thousand U. s.dollars, the value of an increase of 200.4%, the average unit price increased by 48%, an increase of a record high. It is worth mentioning that, only exports of ASEAN tea exports amounted to $1 million 930 thousand, an increase of 50 times. And in Vietnam, Hongkong and other markets for high-end tea, the export unit price is 8-24 times the export of Japanese raw tea.

"Since the Anxi County promoted the construction of the national export tea quality and safety demonstration area, our local farmers' monthly income has increased by about 30%." Anxi County rosin yuan ecological agriculture park general manager Liu Qiuling said, such as rosin yuan ecological agriculture park in the inspection and quarantine department's assistance, was also named the national organic product demonstration base, Fujian outstanding organic tea base, the added value is greatly improved, exports have obvious advantages, is to let local enterprises have high hopes for the overseas market.

It is worth noting that in the Quanzhou inspection and Quarantine Bureau to actively promote the "Anxi mode" role model, is not the only beneficiaries of such a large garden rosin tea enterprises, according to the director of the Quanzhou inspection and Quarantine Bureau, Anxi office Zhang Hua, currently in Anxi, has accounted for half of national ecological tea, organic product demonstration base reached 21. Plus, Anxi in November 2012 was awarded the national export of tea quality safety demonstration area, in October 2015 was awarded the first national organic product certification demonstration area, two pieces of quality and safety of the 'gold' are flowers here, let the local farmers confidence."

Anxi County, as the birthplace of Oolong tea (famous tea), the world famous tea and Tieguanyin tea, has become the biggest livelihood industry and pillar industry in the area. The county tea garden area of 600 thousand mu, total output of 65 thousand tons of tea, tea total output value of 14 billion 800 million yuan, involving a population of about 800000, per capita net income of farmers in 56% from the tea industry, tea farmers become "lifeline", "Qian Daizi". Therefore, from the beginning of September 2010, Quanzhou inspection and Quarantine Bureau and Anxi county government in close cooperation, vigorously promote the export of tea quality and safety demonstration area and the national organic product certification demonstration area construction, to ensure the steady growth of farmers' income.
