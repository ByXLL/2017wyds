A letter to the viewer

Dear browser:

Hello!

Thank you for your busy schedule to take the valuable time to visit this site, this station is honored! The production team welcome your arrival! Next, we will introduce some information about this station.

The site is purely static display, a total of 199 pages, support for responsive browsing.

It's compatible with mainstream browsers, 360 security, speed browsers, Firefox, Google browsers, and so forth.

This site uses the front-end framework, jQuery and Bootstrap, as well as some plug-ins.

The technology used in this station is: HTML5, CSS3, jQuery and so on.

The maximum screen resolution supported by this station is 1920*1080 (large screen computer) with a minimum resolution of 320*480 (iPhone4 screen size).

In order to be compatible with many devices, there are four forms of the station.

The first is the mobile terminal form (cell phone) that appears when the width of the page is less than 768px;

The second is a small screen form (tablet computer) that appears when the width of the page is greater than or equal to 768px and less than 992px;

The third is in the form of a screen (middle screen computer) that appears when the width of the page is greater than or equal to 992px and less than 1200px;

The fourth is a large screen form (large screen computer) that appears when the width of the page is greater than or equal to 1200px;

Note: since the 14 inch and 15 inch laptop screen is larger than 1200px, the site will be displayed in a large screen.
