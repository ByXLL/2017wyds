Yangzhou into the "The Belt and Road" to realize the perfect fusion of ancient culture and modern civilization

2017-08-10 Source: International Online 35

On the afternoon of May 15th, the press conference was held at the Diaoyutai State Guest House in Yangzhou, China's "Silk Road City food culture exchange activities week" in Beijing. Before the start of the conference, Yangzhou mayor Zhu Minyang accepted international online interview, talked about the ancient Yangzhou and Italy fate, Yangzhou in the "The Belt and Road" development opportunities and advantages, perfect and how to let Yangzhou this 2500 history of the ancient city to achieve the fusion of ancient culture and modern civilization.

700 years ago, the world-famous Italy traveler, Mark Polo, came to China from the land Silk Road, creating a bridge between China and Europe with his legend. And he and Yangzhou are inextricably linked, because Marco Polo was in Yangzhou for three years, in his travels in Yangzhou also has detailed records. "Marco Polo can be said to be the forerunner of cultural exchanges between Yangzhou and Italy, said Mayor Zhu Minyang."

He said that since the reform and opening up, Yangzhou continues to accelerate the pace of foreign exchanges, especially because the experience of Marco Polo and Yangzhou, Yangzhou and Italy has Rimini friendly city, and Venice city for the friendly exchanges, and cooperation in many aspects of culture, tourism, sports etc.. At present, there have been many Italy enterprises to invest in Yangzhou, the largest of which is engaged in high precision steel pipe manufacturing Marcegaglia company, a total investment of $250 million.

Jiangsu is the intersection of the development strategy of "The Belt and Road", are also an important part of the Yangtze River Economic Zone, as an important part of Jiangsu, Yangzhou can be said to be an important participant, is the direct beneficiaries. Mayor Zhu Minyang said: "The Belt and Road 'two strategies to bring Yangzhou many development opportunities, such as enterprise, city, talent internationalization opportunities, such as going out and bringing opportunities, such as regional integration development opportunities etc."

He said that the construction industry is a pillar industry in Yangzhou, the basic industry is also rich in people's industry, Yangzhou has a strong competitive and unique. In 2014, Yangzhou's construction industry output value reached 276 billion yuan, an increase of more than 12%. Employing 650 thousand people, of whom one or two construction division reached 16 thousand people. The output value of 93 billion 700 million yuan in the "The Belt and Road" 18 provinces along the Yangtze River economic belt, the output value of 193 billion yuan, completed the $more than 500 million overseas.

Zhu Minyang said, in order to grasp the two strategic opportunities, Yangzhou will accelerate the pace of structural adjustment and transformation and upgrading, construction industrialization forms a complete industrial chain, by strengthening the financial support, and strengthen the various financial and capital cooperation, to create "Yangzhou building" a gilded signboard.

Humanity and ecology are the most distinctive features of Yangzhou and the most important part of Yangzhou's tourism industry. Zhu Minyang said, "ecology is the background of Yangzhou." Yangzhou water area accounted for 1/3 of the city's total area, forest - Wetland coverage of the city's 1/2. Yangzhou has also established the National Forest City and UN Habitat Award city.
